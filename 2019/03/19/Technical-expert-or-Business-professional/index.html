Technical expert or Business professional

Misko Lee's Home Page
2019-03-19

I am often hear a same question from community or friends:

My work is very boring, I want to be a technical expert, I don’t want touch any business logic even seconds.

I really know the psychological background. Beacuse I am think so before also. Technical problem is very excited and clear,

we just need focus on a specific scientific problem, like 1+1=2, but business logic is dirty.

  1. You received frequent changes from clients or Internal PM.

  2. You also don’t know how many hands your code has gone through.

  3. You can’t understand that magic number and hardcode.

  4. You face to a Shit-Mountain everyday, and append new shit on that.

You are very painful for the stupid codebase. You fantasize about being a technologist. just design algorithms,architecture billions web system …

I think technical is easy, bussiness is hard now.

Why?

  1. We almost don’t create any technology,just study and use it. We always is a student. Student is easy,beacuse we just follower genius, never make any new things. But you’re a creator even you build a small blog system, noone can directly help you done the project, you can get more papers if u study or apply a algorithm.
  1. Technology isn’t purpose. I think programmers are waiters for clients/users always. We’re make a solution for solve real workld prolem,make happy to everyone. Technology is just a tool inside the processing.
  1. No,I’m not denying technology. We certainly need to get serious about technology for qualified computer science background. we’re not scientists, enginner is a problem killer, our value is killed big problem,not done a complex existed algorithms.

Please face up to your Shit-Mountain, change it,fix it. make it better just like make self better.
